Biography

Yiming Tang was born in 1982 and received his Ph.D. degree from School of Computer and Information, Hefei University of Technology in 2011. He is now an Associate Professor, Master Tutor in Hefei University of Technology, and is also a visiting professor at University of Alberta, Canada. He is engaged in computer science and mathematics. His current research interests include Clustering, Fuzzy reasoning, Fuzzy system, Social systems, Cognitive system, Machine learning, Image processing, Affective computing, Computer-aided design.
